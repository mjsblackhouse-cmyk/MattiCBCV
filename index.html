With 20 years at the intersection of elite performance, psychology, strategy, and organisational culture, Matti Clements brings to a board what few directors can: a deeply practical, systems-level understanding of how organisations that depend on high-performing people create and protect their value.

As Acting CEO of the Australian Institute of Sport she led sector-wide reform, secured historic federal funding of $300M p.a., and drove Australia's first national high performance strategy — uniting a complex, geographically distributed stakeholder ecosystem behind a shared vision. She holds the AICD Company Directors qualification and has chaired and contributed to nine boards and committees.

Her value is greatest on an already strong board of an organisation where human performance is both the primary asset and the primary source of strategic risk.
